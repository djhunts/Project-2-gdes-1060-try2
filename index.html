The experience

beach campsites

Our shoreline glamp-sites, located right on the sand of the beach, have everything you need. Each weather-proof tent is hotel-room size, complete with 2 beds, a bathroom, and a mini-fridge. Each site has a firepit and beach chairs. Wi-Fi is accessible throughout the entire hotel grounds. Hotel staff will deliver food and items upon request through our website.

dining hall

Our indoor dining hall is where you will likely be eating most of your food. Complementary breakfast, lunch and dinner are served, and a bar is open during and after dinner.

water sports shop

Our water sports shop provides gear and merchandise for sale. Gear may be bought or rented, and includes gear for snorkeling, surfing, paddleboarding, and other beach activities. Merchandise includes clothes, footwear, and other miscellaneous items.
